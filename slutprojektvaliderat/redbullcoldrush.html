Red Bull Cold Rush

Red Bull Cold Rushis the biggest show in backcountry skiing. It is fusing the disciplines of backcountry slopestyle, big mountain, cliffs, and alpine ski touring. A handful of the world's top skiers are invited every year to battle it out for the prizes awarded to the most versatile, well-rounded freeskiers. The competition attracts thousands of people and since 2016 the fans have been brought into action with the People's Choice Award. This award is determined by online voting taking place during the following week.
