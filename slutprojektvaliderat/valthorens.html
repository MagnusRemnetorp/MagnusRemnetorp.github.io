Val Thorens

Val Thorens is the highest ski resort in Europe. It is located in the French Alps. Val Thorens is one of the most attractive ski destinations in Europe because of the three surrounding vallées. The three vallées link the ski area togehter and it results with over 600 km of piste. Val Thorens is therefore the largest linked ski area in the world. Val Thorens was appointed as the world's best ski area year 2013 and 2014 by "World Ski Awards".
