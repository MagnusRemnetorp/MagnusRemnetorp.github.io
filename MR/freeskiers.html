Freeskiing

Freeskiing is a type of alpine skiing. It involves multiple jumps tricks, and terrain park features. These terrain park features are such as rails, boxes, or other obstacles. This form of skiing was first introduced after the growth of snowboarding combined with the progression of freestyle skiing. There are terrain parks, or so called fun/snow parks, are designed specifically for tricks and freeskiing.
